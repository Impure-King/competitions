Introduction + Motivations

Ever since the start of 10th grade, I have feverishly participated in math competitions. It was an extremely ennlightening experience for me, due to the amount of preparation and info-gathering I had to do.

Additionally, it has greatly strengthened my math skills allowing for me to discover creative solutions and enjoy the essense of math. Throughout the journey, I realized that collection information and resources was the key to success. However, not all the information was equally helpful and often wasted more time than actually helped. Therefore, I have currated this list, so that future aspirants can invest more time into problem solving, rather parsing broken links and recorrecting.

The Art of Problem Solving (abbreviated AoPS) has a lot of great problem solving resources that are a must for any math contenstant. It includes a lot of written solutions, previous tests, forums, and even books specially geared towards accelerating learning and ensuring success.
